# LeadHunter Pro

## Version
0.2 (Development)

## Mission

LeadHunter Pro helps agencies, freelancers, and sales teams discover, qualify, and manage high-value local business leads.

Instead of simply finding businesses, LeadHunter Pro analyzes each lead and identifies which businesses are most likely to benefit from marketing, website development, SEO, automation, or AI services.

---

## Target Customers

- SEO Agencies
- Marketing Agencies
- Web Developers
- Cold Calling Teams
- Freelancers
- Sales Teams

---

## Core Features

- Business Search
- Lead Database
- Opportunity Score
- CRM
- CSV / Excel Export
- AI Email Generator
- AI Call Script Generator
- Website Audit

---

## Long-Term Vision

Become the easiest desktop application for finding and qualifying local business leads using AI.
